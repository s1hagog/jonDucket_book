Fender Mustang

The Fender Mustang was introduced in 1964 as the basis of a major redisgn of Fender's student models then consisting of the Musicmaster and Duo-Sonic. It was originally popular in sixties surf music and attained cult status in the 1990s largely as aresult if its use by a number of alternative rock bands.

Fender Stratocaster

The Fender Stratocaster or "Strat" is one of the most popular electric guitars of all time, and its design has been copied bu many guitars makers. It was designed by Leo Fender, George Fullerton and Fredie Tavares in 1954.

Gibson Les Paul

The Gibson Les Paul is solid bodu electric guitar that was first sold in 1952. The Les Paul was designed by Ted McCarthy in colaboration with popular guitarist Les Paul, whom Gibson elisted to endorse the new model. It is one of the most well-known electric guitar types in the world.
